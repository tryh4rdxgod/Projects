TAIV ACCOUNT TRACKER STUDIO - PROJECT PITCH

I built this polished account tracker as a practical product demo for Taiv.

What it shows:
- I understand account coordination
- I understand CRM hygiene
- I understand follow-up discipline
- I understand that sales/account teams need clean next steps
- I can think in systems, not just tasks
- I can build something usable and presentable

How I would explain it:

"I built a small Taiv-inspired account tracker to show how I think about account
coordination and sales operations. It tracks venues, contacts, status, priority,
revenue opportunity, owner, campaign notes and follow-up dates. The dashboard highlights
pipeline value, hot accounts and due follow-ups. It is not meant to replace Taiv's
real CRM; it is a practical demo showing how I organize account data and reduce missed
client actions."

Best interview line:

"Give me a process, a CRM and a busy sales/account team - I can help keep account data
clean, follow-ups visible, notes organized and execution moving."
