
TAIV ACCOUNT TRACKER - PROJECT PITCH

I built this small C++ desktop application as a practical demo for Taiv.

The goal is not to replace a full CRM. The goal is to show how I think about account
coordination and sales operations:

- Clean account information
- Clear account status
- Follow-up dates
- Priority tracking
- Revenue opportunity visibility
- Campaign notes
- Owner visibility
- Reduced missed follow-ups

WHY THIS MATTERS FOR TAIV
Taiv works with venues, advertisers, campaigns, follow-ups, and account performance.
A simple tracking tool like this demonstrates the same operating logic:

1. Keep every account organized.
2. Know the next step.
3. Know who owns the account.
4. Keep campaign/client notes clear.
5. Reduce missed follow-ups.
6. Make sales/account work easier to manage.

HOW I WOULD EXPLAIN IT
"I made this project to show that I understand more than basic admin work.
I understand that account teams need clean data, clear follow-ups, and simple systems
that make client work easier. I used C++ to build a small Windows desktop tracker that
stores account data, tracks priorities, and highlights follow-ups due today or earlier."
